 ### Amazon EC2

  -EC2 is one of the most popular of AWS's offering
  -EC2 = Elastic Computer Cloud = **Infrastructure as a Service**
  -It mainly consist in the capablity of:
      1.Renting virtual machines (EC2 instances)
      2.Storing data on virtual drives(EBS)
      3.Distributing load across machines (ELB)
      4.Scalin the services using an auto-scaling group (ASG)
